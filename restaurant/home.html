Bee Bee's started as a small divebar in the Caribbean. Ben R. and Becky L. started the bar together after they were ship wrecked and stranded in the middle of paradise. The couple learned how to fish, catch shrimp, and built a make-shift bar out of driftwood found on Eagle Beach. You know what they say... if you build it, they will come. And they did.

Within one year, Ben and Becky made enough money to fly home to California and start a tropical themed bourbon bar. Thanks to the help from the mayor of Arooba, Tom Nook, the couple were able to build one of the flashiest bars in the world. Ben and Becky are now indebted to Tom Nook for the rest of their lives. Welcome to Bee Bee's!
